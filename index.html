SHOULD YOU CLEAN YOUR OLD HOUSE AFTER MOVING?

Looking at your moving checklist can easily give you a headache. There are so many tasks on it! One of the last being to clean your old house after moving. While it’s not something everyone does, it certainly should be completed. So, after your Dynamic Movers NYC leave, and you have an empty house, it’s time to start cleaning. It may be a tedious process, and a time-consuming one, but we’ve got a bunch of tips for you to make it easier.

Cleaning doesn’t have to be a difficult task if you do it right.

Clean your old house after moving for many reasons

Whether you are renting or selling, there are many benefits to cleaning your old place. Unfortunately, it’s not as common as it should be. Many people leave behind piles of trash and dirty surfaces. Moving to a new house is complicated enough on its own. If you have to deep clean your new home before the local movers arrive with your belongings, it makes it even more complicated and hectic. Don’t be that guy, and let your new owners or tenants into a clean home.

Moreover, if you leave behind a dirty apartment or a house, your landlord might not give you back your security deposit. As we all know, tracking your moving budget is very important, and every dollar counts. A few hours of cleaning will give you that money back! On the other hand, if you are planning to put the property on the market, it will be much more appealing to potential buyers in a clean and neat condition.

The new tenant or a buyer will appreciate arriving to a clean home.

Don’t have the time to clean? Hire a professional team!

Much like you can get complete moving service NYC, you can also hire a crew to come and scrub the house for you. This is still a better option than leaving it dirty for the next family that’s moving in. Arrange for them to come when most of your stuff is already packed since it will be much faster and easier for them to complete the job. Sure, it costs a bit of money, but it will save you a deposit and a potential buyer! It’s a good idea to do this a few days before your moving day, in case anything falls through.

Cleaning supplies you’ll need for the job

In case you’ve decided to clean your old house after moving yourself, here’s a good tip. Gather all the cleaning supplies on time, so you don’t have to waste time making multiple trips to the store! Here are just a few basic things you’ll need:

      • Microfiber cloths

      • Multi-surface cleaning spray

      • Degreaser

      • Rubber gloves

      • Glass cleaner

      • A mop and a vacuum cleaner

Purchase all of your cleaning supplies before you begin this task.

And that’s pretty much it! We hope we convinced you and showed you that cleaning your old place before you relocate is quite an important task. With a bit of help from your family members (or the professionals), it can be done in no time.
